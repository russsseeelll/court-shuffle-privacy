Privacy Policy for Court Shuffle

Last updated: 2 December 2025

Court Shuffle is a mobile application designed to help organize racket sport sessions such as tennis and badminton. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Court Shuffle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data used by the app, including player names, sessions, matches, and related information, is stored locally on your device.

Specifically, the app:

  • Does not create an account or require registration
  • Does not collect your name, email address, or contact details
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Local Data on Your Device

Any information you enter into Court Shuffle (such as player names or session details) is stored only on your device's local storage. You are responsible for managing, backing up, or deleting this data through your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Court Shuffle does not collect any personal information from users, including children. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about how the app works, you can contact us using the details below.

Third-Party Services

Court Shuffle does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sent to external servers for processing or storage.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
